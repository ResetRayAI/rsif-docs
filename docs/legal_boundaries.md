# Legal Boundaries

RSIF public documentation describes technical imaging concepts only.

---

# RSIF is NOT intended for:

- diagnosis;
- disease detection;
- disease classification;
- treatment recommendation;
- clinical decision support;
- emergency interpretation;
- standalone medical conclusions.

---

# Technical Scope

RSIF documentation may describe:

- CT attenuation terminology;
- ROI concepts;
- structured imaging semantics;
- quantitative imaging concepts;
- anonymized imaging exports;
- AI-readable imaging structures.

---

# Public Documentation Notice

Public RSIF repositories contain documentation and semantic concepts only.

They do not contain:

- production medical workflows;
- internal AI logic;
- proprietary validation systems;
- clinical inference systems;
- protected implementation details.

---

# Responsibility Boundary

Interpretation of medical imaging remains the responsibility of qualified medical professionals.
