# AI Interoperability

RSIF is designed as a semantic layer for AI-readable imaging workflows.

---

# Goals

The RSIF semantic structure aims to support:

- structured imaging exchange;
- AI-readable imaging concepts;
- machine-readable terminology;
- interoperability between imaging systems;
- structured quantitative imaging workflows.

---

# AI-readable concepts

RSIF may describe:

- quantitative CT terminology;
- ROI-related structures;
- structured imaging metadata;
- semantic imaging mappings;
- anonymized technical exports.

---

# Public Documentation Scope

Public repositories contain semantic documentation only.

They do not expose:

- internal orchestration logic;
- proprietary AI workflows;
- production pipelines;
- matching systems;
- threshold logic;
- validation algorithms.

---

# Interoperability Focus

RSIF focuses on semantic interoperability rather than clinical inference.
